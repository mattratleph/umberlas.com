Matt Ratleph

Matt is one of the co-founders of Umberlas and he lives in Wheaton, IL. He makes ambient and downtempo with a glitchy flavor. He also collaborates with Trato Stat for the Umberlas Community Project releases.

Trato Stat

Trato Stat is Billy Battle, one of the co-founders of Umberlas. He lives in Naperville, IL and he makes experimental acoustic music. He also collaborates with Matt Ratleph for the Umberlas Community Project releases.
