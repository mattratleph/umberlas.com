UCP 1: Synergy

The first Umberlas Community Project release. This was a while in the making, containing the first track Matt Ratleph and Billy Battle ever collaborated on. There's also the original concept beat, all eight minutes of it, that eventually became that first track. The release is rounded out but some previously unreleased tracks from both Matt and Billy.
